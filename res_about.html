About Us

Introducing our newest culinary venture, a restaurant that embodies our love and passion for exceptional cuisine – where every dish is meticulously crafted with care. From the moment you walk through our doors, you will be enveloped in an ambiance that speaks volumes about our dedication to a memorable dining experience. What sets us apart from the rest is not just our commitment to delivering delectable flavors, but also the diverse and accomplished team of chefs we have assembled from various corners of the globe. Their expertise and years of experience shine through in each and every bite, making your visit an unforgettable journey for your palate. Join us and allow us to take you on an unparalleled culinary adventure, where every recipe is prepared with an abundance of love and passion.

Sanjeev Kapoor

With 12 years of experience in mastering the art of French cuisine, our talented chef hailing all the way from Canada brings a touch of elegance to our dishes. With a deep understanding of French flavors and culinary techniques, he flawlessly combines traditional techniques with his own creative twist, guaranteeing a delightful experience for our guests seeking an exquisite dining experience.

Uncle Roger

Our skilled chef from Telangana holds a rich 6 years of experience specializing in the vibrant and aromatic South Indian cuisine. Having grown up with the authentic flavors of the region, he expertly creates dishes that transport our patrons straight to the heart of South India. From crispy dosas to flavorful biryanis, he has mastered the intricate balance of spices and ingredients that make South Indian cuisine truly special.

Vikas Khanna

Our talented chef from Delhi boasts 2 years of experience in crafting delectable dishes from the diverse and rich tapestry of North Indian cuisine. With every plate he presents, he showcases the complexity and depth of flavors found in dishes like butter chicken, kebabs, and biryanis. His passion for regional Indian cuisine shines through each dish, giving our guests a taste of the diverse culinary heritage of Delhi and its surroundings.
